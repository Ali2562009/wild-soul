Who I am

I left. Not dramatically — no goodbye letter, no big announcement. I just walked out into something slower, wilder, and more honest than anything the modern world was offering me.

"The forest doesn't ask you to prove yourself. Neither does the river. They just let you be."

I'm a Muslim, but not the kind who carries religion like a burden. I carry it like a quiet light — it's there when I sit still enough to notice it, in the sound of water, in a sky full of stars, in the small ridiculous things that make life funny and good.

This website is where I write those things down. The adventures. The embarrassing moments. The ideas that came to me sitting under a tree. The simple things that somehow changed everything.

What this place is about

Four things I live by

01

Wild & free

Nature is the best teacher, the best therapist, and the best comedian. I live in it as much as I can and write down what it tells me.

02

Quiet faith

No preaching. No pressure. Just a Muslim heart trying to live gently, honestly, and with gratitude for this strange, beautiful world.

03

Real laughter

Life is funny. Deeply, absurdly funny. The donkey that wouldn't move. The rain that came at exactly the wrong moment. I write it all down.

04

Honest ideas

Not advice from a guru. Just thoughts from someone sitting under a tree — about how to live better, lighter, and with less noise.

"The world will always be loud. You don't have to be."
